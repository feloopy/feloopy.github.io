ODL Live is an extremely flexible, low-cost vehicle route optimisation engine supporting multi-day route optimisation, next-day optimisation, same-day/realtime route optimisation and on-demand as well as route-efficient appointment booking.

ODL Live is suitable for planning many different types of routing problems, including deliveries, passenger services, and mobile workforce management. It has also been tailored to optimise non-emergency medical transportation.

Self-hosting installation available, as webserver(s) or command line tool.
